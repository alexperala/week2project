Acting

Dave has appeared in commercials for Mio, Canadian Tire and the Ontario Film Review Board, and the film Dead of Winter.

Stand-Up

Dave can be seen doing standup comedy throughout Toronto at venues such as the Rivoli and the Comedy Bar.

Theatre

Dave is a founding member of Players Players, a theatre collective and sketch troupe responsible for the fringe hits DOGS! The Musical!! (‘The best dog-gone musical at the Fringe’ – The Toronto Star) and Science Fair! (***** - Eye Weekly; NNNN – Now Magazine).

Writing

In addition to the material he writes for his stand-up and his vaudeville duo, Parker & Seville, Dave has also written jokes for The Score.

What's his deal? Trying to be funny in any way possible, but mostly through acting on film, standup comedy and sketch comedy with well-regarded comedy duo Parker and Seville.

TV and Film

Dave has co-starred in a series of commercials for Mio, appeared in a Canadian Tire gift card ad, and also appeared in the feature film Dead of Winter. In all cases he nailed it.

On Stage

Dave has appeared on stage doing his own stand-up shows, comedy brawls, as part of his Parker & Seville vaudeville duo, and he's also acted as part of a mystery dinner theater, which wasn't really on stage but was still live, so.
